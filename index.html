      <h2>Research</h2>

      <p>

        I am a sociolinguist and experimental researcher interested in how language shapes — and is shaped by — social bias and cognition. 

        My work focuses on three areas:

      </p>

      <ul>

        <li><strong>Accent-based hiring bias</strong>: how listeners’ evaluations of job candidates are influenced by accent, gender, and ethnicity, with a focus on French contexts.</li>

        <li><strong>Phonological plasticity</strong>: how adults adapt to new dialects and accents through experience and social exposure.</li>

        <li><strong>Language and animal agency</strong>: how subtle linguistic choices (e.g. pronouns, relativizers) influence how people attribute agency to animals and make decisions about meat consumption.</li>

      </ul>
